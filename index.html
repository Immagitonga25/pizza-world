About Us

Pizza World is a family-owned company founded in 2005 in Nairobi, Kenya.
We are made up of a dedicated team that strives to serve you a variety of freshly made homemade pizza.
Our pizzas are made with the finest fresh ingredients and are cooked in brick ovens to give you that unique smoky flavor, crunchy toppings and a wonderful fluffy crust!
We will truly rock your world!
